# Doña Carmen Mendoza

**Role:** Member · Philanthropy lead
**Generation:** 2 (by marriage)
**Council seat:** Yes, since 2014
**Born:** 1951, Cebu

## Stewardship summary

Doña Carmen has run the philanthropic side of the Mendoza family since 2014. The [[mendoza-foundation]] reports to her. She holds an informal veto on any commitment over the foundation's annual allocation; the council has never overridden her on a philanthropic question and is unlikely to.

## Council relationships

- Spouse of [[don-tomas-mendoza]]
- Mother of [[isabel-mendoza-reyes]] and [[marco-mendoza]]

## Active commitments

- Annual review of the foundation's grant cadence — typically Q4
- Co-signs all foundation distributions above ₱50M
