# Marco Mendoza

**Role:** Heir · Operating lead, [[casa-mendoza-consumer]]
**Generation:** 3 (younger son of Don Tomás)
**Born:** 1981, Manila

## Stewardship summary

Marco has held operating responsibility for the family's boutique consumer brand since 2019. He does not currently hold a council seat. The trajectory the family expects is that Marco takes a council seat following the next council expansion (anticipated 2028) once the operating record across two cycles is established.

He is the operational lead Atrium's commercial pilot is being run with.
