# Isabel's council appointment · February 2026

**Type:** Succession marker (council seat)
**Outgoing:** None (council expansion, not replacement)
**Incoming:** [[isabel-mendoza-reyes]]
**Effective:** 2026-02-14

## What happened

The family council expanded from four seats to five with Isabel's appointment in February 2026. She is the first generation-3 family member to hold a council seat in the Mendoza family's recorded history.

## Don Tomás's reasoning, preserved

Captured by Maria in a working session 2026-02-12, two days before the appointment:

> "Isabel has read the trust documents. Marco has not. That is not a judgment of either of them — Marco runs an operating unit and reads what he needs to. Isabel reads what no one is asking her to read. That is the council habit, and the council needs it before we are ready to lose anyone."

> "If we wait until succession forces our hand, we will be choosing under pressure. We will not be choosing the right thing. We are choosing now while there is nothing to lose by choosing."

> "Marco's chair comes when his operating record speaks for itself. That is two more cycles. He knows it. We have spoken."

## What this means for the next generation

Isabel's first council session was 2026-05-15. The next-generation council briefing on 2026-05-22 is the first sitting where she is present as a seated member and Marco is present as a non-seat attendee.

The next succession marker the family expects to record is Marco's seat in 2028.
