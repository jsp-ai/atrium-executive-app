# Mendoza Family Trust

**Settlor:** Lolo Salvador Mendoza (1991, deceased 2018)
**Governing law:** Philippines (with Singapore amendments per 2024)
**Trustees:**
- Bank of the Philippine Islands · Trust & Asset Management (institutional trustee)
- Atty. Antonio Salazar (individual trustee)

**Beneficiaries by class:**
- Class A — Don Tomás Mendoza (lifetime income)
- Class B — Doña Carmen Mendoza (lifetime income on Don Tomás's predecease)
- Class C — Isabel Mendoza-Reyes, Marco Mendoza (capital on the survivor's death)
- Class D — Lineal descendants of Class C (per stirpes)

## Active matters

- [[q2-distribution-mendoza-trust]] — Friday 2026-05-24 · Don Tomás's signature pending
- 2024 Singapore amendments — class language under review by counsel; see [[singapore-sfo-licensing]] for the related restructuring

## Why the trust is structured this way

Lolo Salvador's reasoning, in his own words from the 1991 settlement memorandum:

> "The lineal classes exist because the family will outlive the businesses. The businesses exist because the family lives in them. Build the trust to survive both."

Atrium captured a fuller working session with Don Tomás about the 1991 framing on 2026-05-09; it is filed under [[founder-1989-textile-exit]] (the textile exit and the trust settlement happened in adjacent years and are referenced together by the family).
