# Mendoza Holdings, Inc.

**Jurisdiction:** Philippines
**Registered:** 1973
**Capital structure:** Closely held; principal beneficial owner is the [[mendoza-family-trust]]

## Position summary

Mendoza Holdings is the top-level operating shell that controls all family business interests in the Philippines. Lolo Salvador established it in 1973 as the vehicle to consolidate three textile-era holdings into a single succession-friendly entity. The 1989 textile exit was executed through Mendoza Holdings; see [[exit-textiles-1989]] for the founder's preserved reasoning.

Current operating units:

- [[casa-mendoza-consumer]] — boutique consumer brand, operating lead Marco
- Mendoza Logistics (Manila) — operating lead Rico Salazar (non-family)
- Mendoza Properties (Cebu, Pasig) — operating lead Ana Villar (non-family)

## Governance

- Chair: Don Tomás Mendoza
- Vice-chair: vacant (since Lolo Salvador's death 2018)
- Independent director: Atty. Antonio Salazar (also family counsel)
- Annual general meeting: every March

## Why the family will not sell

Three serious offers have come and gone, the most recent in 2024. The reasoning the family returns to is recorded across multiple decision pages — see [[exit-textiles-1989]] for the founding precedent and Don Tomás's framings.
