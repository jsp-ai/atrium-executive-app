# Casa Mendoza Consumer

**Jurisdiction:** Philippines
**Registered:** 2003
**Parent:** [[mendoza-holdings]]
**Operating lead:** [[marco-mendoza]]

## What it is

The family's boutique consumer brand — premium dry goods, regional distribution. Founded 2003 by Don Tomás as a vehicle to give Marco an operating chair after his MBA. Third-generation operating record begins here.

This is the operating unit where Atrium's commercial pilot is running, week 3 of Install at time of writing. The wiki, the schema, and the weekly briefing format you are reading were calibrated here first; they will extend to the rest of [[mendoza-holdings]] beginning Q3 2026.
