# Mendoza Foundation

**Established:** 1998
**Lead:** [[dona-carmen-mendoza]]
**Annual cadence:** Q4 grant review · Q1 commitments · Q2-Q3 disbursement

## What it does

The Mendoza Foundation supports two areas: textile-history preservation in the Philippines (an explicit continuity with the family's pre-1989 operating life) and tertiary education for women in family-business succession contexts.

The foundation is funded by an annual transfer from the [[mendoza-family-trust]] (Class A income) and operates with a 3% asset-base annual disbursement target.

## Why the foundation exists at all

Lolo Salvador's framing, preserved by Doña Carmen:

> "The textile business is gone. The textile families are not. The girls who would have been textile heiresses still exist. They are not heiresses now."

## What is not in scope

The foundation does not make political donations, does not fund individual scholarships outside the women's succession program, and does not co-fund with corporate entities.
