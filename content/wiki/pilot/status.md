# First commercial pilot — live.

**Customer:** A family-held consumer brand, third generation. Under NDA.

**Started:** May 1, 2026. Currently week 3 of Install.

**Areas live:** Finance, operations. Family-council briefing format onboarding next.

**Wiki size:** 84 pages and growing. Schema covers products, suppliers, family stakeholders, capital events, recurring decisions, and the rolling 13-week cash forecast.

**First win:** Day 9. The principal asked Atrium what the latest decision was on a contested EU distributor question. Atrium composed the answer from three Slack threads, one council memo, and a counsel email — with citations. The CFO had been about to re-litigate the same question with the family.

**Case study cadence:** 30 / 60 / 90 days. First case study publishes mid-June.

This is the proof point Atrium has been waiting for. Until first commercial run, Atrium was an architectural argument. After first commercial run, it is a practice whose value compounds week over week.
