# Where Atrium is today.

The practice is in its first commercial year. The proof points are deliberately concrete.

## Three personal vaults of the founder

Running this exact architecture across executive operating, financial governance, and long-form creative practice. Years of compounded institutional memory queryable in plain language. The founder eats his own cooking.

## The Implementation Manual

100+ pages — the working artifact of a system in production use. Documents the macOS workstation setup, the project schema, the workflow loop, the troubleshooting table, and the file templates. Released by request to qualified inquiries.

## First commercial pilot live, May 2026

A family-held consumer brand, third generation. Under NDA. Week three of Install at time of writing. 84 wiki pages and growing. First win on day nine — composed a contested-decision rollup from three Slack threads, one council memo, and a counsel email, with citations, before the principal re-litigated the question.

## Case-study cadence

30 / 60 / 90 days from each Install. First case study publishes mid-June 2026.

---

This is the proof point Atrium has been waiting for. Until first commercial run, Atrium was an architectural argument. After first commercial run, it is a practice whose value compounds week over week.
