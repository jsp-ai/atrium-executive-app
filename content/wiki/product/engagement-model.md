# Three tiers. One entry point.

Atrium engagements come in three shapes. All three are entered through the same one-week diagnostic.

## Atrium Install — one-time, ~6 weeks

Set up the stack. Write the schema. Train the executive team or family council. Onboard the first three areas of the operation. The build.

## Atrium Operate — monthly retainer

Schema maintenance. Lint reviews. Ingest service operation. Executive coaching. Monthly synthesis pass. The partnership.

## Atrium Custom — project-based

Department-specific schemas. Integrations with specific operational systems. Training cohorts. The bespoke.

Pricing scales with the size and complexity of the operation — discussed in the [[one-week-diagnostic]]. It is not published.

Most relationships start at Install, continue into Operate, and occasionally extend into Custom. The product is designed to compound: each month of Operate increases the wiki's value to the family or company. Atrium does not sell software seats. It sells a deepening stewardship of the family's institutional memory.
